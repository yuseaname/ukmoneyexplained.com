## Our Mission

UK Money Explained exists to make personal finance accessible to everyone in the United Kingdom. We believe that understanding your money shouldn't require a degree in economics.

Too many people make costly financial decisions — not because they're careless, but because the information available is buried in jargon, complex regulations, and impenetrable small print. We're here to change that.

## What We Do

We publish clear, accurate explanations of UK finance topics including:

- **Credit & Debt** — How credit scores work, what affects them, and how to manage debt
- **Loans & Borrowing** — APR explained, types of loans, and how repayments work
- **Mortgages** — How to get on the property ladder, mortgage types, and affordability
- **Taxes** — Income tax bands, National Insurance, council tax, and stamp duty
- **Household Bills** — What average bills cost and how to reduce them
- **Tools & Calculators** — Free tools to help you work out costs and compare options

## Our Approach

Every article on UK Money Explained follows the same principles:

1. **Plain English** — No jargon. If we use a technical term, we explain it.
2. **UK-specific** — All our content is written specifically for the UK market, using UK tax bands, UK regulations, and UK-specific services.
3. **Fact-checked** — We reference official sources including gov.uk, the Office for National Statistics (ONS), the Bank of England, and the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA).
4. **Regularly updated** — Financial information changes. We review and update our content to ensure accuracy, particularly around tax year changes.
5. **No financial advice** — We explain how things work. We do not tell you what to do with your money. For personal financial advice, always consult a qualified financial adviser.
